Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Mataha Maze Garden

What are your opening hours?

Mataha Maze Garden is open daily from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Last admission is at 5:00 PM. We're open year-round, though hours may vary seasonally. Special evening events are scheduled throughout the year.

Is the maze suitable for young children?

Yes! Our maze is designed to be family-friendly. We have a special 'Mini Maze' area for children under 6, and the main maze is appropriate for children 7 and up when accompanied by an adult. We also offer stroller-friendly paths throughout the garden.

Do I need to book tickets in advance?

While walk-ins are welcome, we strongly recommend booking in advance, especially for weekends and special events. Advance booking guarantees entry and often comes with a small discount. You can book tickets online through our website or by calling our booking line.

Are pets allowed in the maze garden?

We love animals, but to ensure the safety and comfort of all visitors, only service animals are permitted inside the maze garden. We have shaded parking areas where pets can wait comfortably, and water bowls are available at the entrance.

What happens if it rains during my visit?

The maze is an outdoor experience, but we offer rain checks for inclement weather. If heavy rain occurs during your visit, you may request a rain check to return another day. We also have covered picnic areas and a visitor center where you can wait out light rain.
